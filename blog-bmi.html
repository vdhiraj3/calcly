How to Calculate BMI Correctly

Published on Jan 24, 2025 • 5 min read

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation that helps you understand your health based on height and weight...

The Formula

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m)²

Healthy Ranges

  • Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30 or more
